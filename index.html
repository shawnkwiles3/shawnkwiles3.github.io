    The Amber Ridge Experience


    Attention bird-watchers and outdoor enthusiasts: This beautiful South Texas RV Park features 47 wooded acres of wildlife area. Come visit us and experience one of our 46 Cabins and Cottages or one of our RV and tent sites. Enjoy hiking and swimming in our pool. Keep an eye out for arrowheads; this site is a Karankawa Indian state archaeological landmark. It’s a great central location for day trips to San Antonio, Gulf of Mexico, Padre Island National Seashore, Texas State Aquarium, Mexico and more. This location is perfect for your family reunion or rally. Scheduled activities from November 20 to March 1. WiFi is available. What more could a weary overnighter ask for? You may decide to stay a month or two
